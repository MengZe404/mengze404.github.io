## Research Ideas
- To improve existing models (e.g. ARIMA) for making better predictions in the financial market. (current)
- To explore the application of DNNs and RNNs in price prediction (e.g. LTSM). (current)
- To propose a robust machine learning model that is suitable for portfolio management involving multidimensional data.
- To develop an auto-trading program based on NLP, Deep Learning and financial modelling, aiming to capture the market volatility based on textual information and numerical data.
